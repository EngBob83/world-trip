Why this journey

After a long stretch of hard weather, I am setting out to see the people who have shaped me before another season slips by. The dates are loose by design — I want each of you to choose the window that fits, not the other way around.

Each stop on this chart is a chapter. Your chapter. If you are reading this, you made the map for a reason — and I mean to make the time count.

— Robert
